— About

NIPUN Bharat is India's national mission to ensure that every child attains foundational literacy and numeracy by the end of Grade 3.

This page documents the on-ground work behind that goal — the classrooms visited, the data systems designed, the teachers coached, and the decisions made block by block, district by district.

A practitioner's approach to foundational learning.

Three pillars
01 / Classroom

Teaching that meets learners

Structured pedagogy, paced lesson plans, and assessment-led remediation that honours where each child actually is.

02 / System

Government, working alongside

Block-level review rhythms, district dashboards, and the slow, patient work of building shared accountability across cadres.

03 / Data

Evidence in everyone's hands

Chatbot-driven assessments, BigQuery pipelines, and dashboards that turn classroom signal into decisions teachers can act on.
